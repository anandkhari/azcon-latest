BUILDING MAINTENANCE WORKS
Building maintenance ensures the safety, functionality, and longevity of facilities. Our services include preventive, corrective, and emergency maintenance across MEP systems, civil works, HVAC, firefighting, and water supply systems.

Our main services in building maintenance include:
- Routine inspection, servicing, and preventive maintenance of MEP systems
- Repair and replacement of electrical and plumbing fixtures
- Maintenance of HVAC, firefighting, and water supply systems
- Civil and structural maintenance, including painting, flooring, and waterproofing
- Facade cleaning and glass restoration for commercial and high-rise buildings
- Annual maintenance contracts (AMCs) for residential and commercial properties
- Emergency response and corrective maintenance
- Comprehensive reporting, documentation, and 24/7 customer support

FIT-OUT WORKS (RESIDENTIAL & COMMERCIAL)
Azcon provides professional residential and commercial fit-out services focused on quality, functionality, and craftsmanship.

Our main services in fit-out works include:
- Interior design development, space planning, and detailed drawings
- Partition, ceiling, flooring, and wall finishing works
- Joinery and carpentry works, including custom-made furniture
- Electrical lighting and data cabling installation
- HVAC installation and integration
- Plumbing, sanitary, and drainage systems

- Coordination with civil and MEP services
- Testing, commissioning, and after-sales maintenance

HVAC WORKS
HVAC technology is essential for creating comfortable indoor environments. Our HVAC services cover design, installation, testing, and maintenance of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems.

Our main services in HVACs are as follows:
- Engineering services including heat load calculation, system design, and equipment selection
- Procurement of materials as per approved technical lists
- Installation of ducting, chilled water piping, and accessories
- Air balancing and chiller water balancing
- Installation of ventilation systems, including staircases pressurization, smoke exhaust, and car park ventilation
- Heating plant solutions, including boilers, chillers, and heat exchangers
- Comprehensive testing, commissioning, and after-sales services

INFRASTRUCTURE MAINTENANCE
We provide comprehensive infrastructure maintenance solutions to support the long-term performance, safety, and sustainability of assets.

ROADS & PAVEMENTS
- Routine inspections of internal roads, curbstones, footpaths, and driveways
- Asphalt crack sealing, repairs, and resurfacing
- Remarking of road markings, traffic signage, and parking layouts
- Replacement of damaged asphalt, tiles, or interlock paving
- Repair of speed humps, water barriers, and expansion joints

DRAINAGE & SEWER NETWORKS

- High-pressure jetting and CCTV inspection of pipelines
- Repair and replacement of damaged or collapsed drainage pipes

POTABLE WATER & IRRIGATION SYSTEMS
- Inspection of potable water lines, chambers, and valves
- Leak detection, pressure monitoring, and flushing

LANDSCAPING & SOFTSCAPE

WASTE & ENVIRONMENTAL SERVICES
